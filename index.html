Assassin's Creed

Assassin’s Creed (2007) was developed by Ubisoft and set during the Third Crusade (1191 AD), bringing players into the world of stealth, parkour, and historical intrigue. Step into the shoes of Altaïr Ibn-La'Ahad, a master assassin on a mission to eliminate key Templar targets and restore balance to the Holy Land. Through the Animus, modern-day Desmond Miles relives his ancestor’s past, uncovering the hidden war between Assassins and Templars. With immersive open-world exploration and dynamic gameplay, the legend begins here. Are you ready to embrace the Creed?

Assassin's Creed II

Assassin’s Creed II (2009) was released by Ubisoft, elevating the franchise to legendary status with its rich storytelling and breathtaking world. Set in the heart of the Italian Renaissance, it follows Ezio Auditore da Firenze, a young nobleman whose world is shattered by betrayal. From a reckless youth to a master assassin, Ezio embarks on a path of vengeance, uncovering an ancient war between the Assassins and Templars. Explore the vibrant streets of Florence, Venice, and Rome, scale towering cathedrals, and forge alliances with icons like Leonardo da Vinci. With refined parkour, deadly combat, and a world teeming with secrets, Assassin’s Creed II isn’t just a game—it’s a masterpiece of history, adventure, and destiny.

Assassin's Creed Brotherhood

Assassin’s Creed: Brotherhood (2010) was released by Ubisoft, taking the franchise to new heights with its rich narrative, breathtaking world, and evolved gameplay. Set in the heart of Renaissance Rome, the game follows Ezio Auditore da Firenze, now a master Assassin, as he wages war against the tyrannical Borgia family. With the city under Templar control, Ezio must rebuild the Assassin Brotherhood, recruit and train new warriors, and strike from the shadows to restore freedom. Rome becomes his battlefield—its towering landmarks, bustling streets, and hidden passageways alive with danger and intrigue. Featuring refined combat, fluid parkour, strategic Brotherhood management, and the franchise’s first multiplayer mode, Assassin’s Creed: Brotherhood isn’t just a game—it’s a revolution.

Assassin's Creed Revelations

Assassin’s Creed: Revelations (2011) was released by Ubisoft, delivering a breathtaking finale to Ezio Auditore’s legendary journey. Now an aging Master Assassin, Ezio travels to Constantinople, the heart of the Ottoman Empire, in search of the lost Keys to Masyaf, unlocking the long-buried secrets of Altaïr Ibn-La'Ahad. As the Templars tighten their grip on the city, Ezio must embrace new tools like the Hookblade for faster parkour and master the art of bomb crafting to outmaneuver his enemies. Meanwhile, Desmond Miles, trapped in the depths of the Animus, fights to piece together his fractured mind. With an emotional narrative, stunning historical landscapes, and refined gameplay, Assassin’s Creed: Revelations is not just a game—it is the ultimate convergence of past, present, and destiny.

Assassin's Creed III

Assassin’s Creed III (2012) was released by Ubisoft, bringing the franchise to the heart of the American Revolution. Set in the late 18th century, the game follows Ratonhnhaké:ton, also known as Connor, a half-Mohawk, half-English Assassin fighting to protect his people and secure freedom for a new nation. From the bustling streets of Boston and New York to the vast, untamed Frontier, Connor uses his lethal combat skills, parkour, and stealth to hunt down the Templars who seek control over the revolution. With dynamic weather, naval warfare, and an evolving world shaped by war and politics, Assassin’s Creed III delivers a powerful, action-packed journey through one of history’s most defining moments.

Assassin's Creed IV Black Flag

Assassin’s Creed IV: Black Flag (2013) was released by Ubisoft, taking the franchise to the golden age of piracy. Set in the early 18th century Caribbean, the game follows Edward Kenway, a ruthless pirate-turned-Assassin who navigates the deadly waters between freedom and loyalty. From the bustling ports of Havana, Kingston, and Nassau to the open seas filled with naval battles, treasure hunts, and hidden secrets, Edward carves his own path in a world ruled by empires and outlaws. With breathtaking naval combat, seamless open-world exploration, and a story of betrayal, brotherhood, and the pursuit of power, Assassin’s Creed IV: Black Flag is the ultimate pirate adventure, where the creed meets the sea.
